About Me

I'm a tech enthusiast with a passion for innovation, cloud computing, and building solutions that make a real impact. My journey in tech started at age 12, experimenting with Scratch, and quickly evolved into more complex projects — including a student ID-based card payment system I created in high school, which earned me the Best IT Final Project of the Year.

I’m currently pursuing my goal of becoming a Solution Engineer or Cloud Architect, blending strong technical skills with excellent communication, attention to detail, and a natural curiosity for how things work. I stay up to date with the latest tech trends, learn fast, and thrive in dynamic, challenge-driven environments.

Outside of tech, I’m also a passionate barista, bringing the same care and creativity to every coffee as I do to every line of code. Whether I’m working on an app or crafting the perfect espresso, my goal is the same — to create meaningful, thoughtful experiences for people.
